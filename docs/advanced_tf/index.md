# Advanced TensorFlow 2.0 w/ Keras

Google's open sourced TensorFlow, a top project on the ~36 million developers' GitHub, is arguably one of the most successful applications of the mathematics we understand and personally came to love. This amazingly capable, community developed tool suddenly allows us to simulate and study our insights and understanding of the reality around us.

The new, "cleaned up" version, the beta of TensorFlow 2.0, was just released with its APIs now frozen. Personally motivated and eagerly curious to "read the sources", the following blogs document our process of learning TensorFlow 2.0, without reading the documentation:
